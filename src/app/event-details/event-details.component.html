PAINT WITH A PURPOSE

In our ongoing commitment to give back to our local communities, Painting with a Twist® proudly sponsors a monthly fund raising events – Painting with a Purpose. Proceeds from Painting with a Purpose events are donated to local non-profit organizations. Painting with a Twist has donated over 4 million dollars to local non-profit organizations such as Habitat for Humanity, Multiple Sclerosis and Odyssey House. We are committed to giving back to the local community, one painting at at time.

We're excited to see you in the studio! Please arrive 15 min before class so we can welcome you to the studio, show you around, answer any questions and make sure you're comfortable in your seat. You are always welcome to come early, stay late and enjoy the studio or our outside patio .We offer local wines and craft beer as well as a full menu at affordable prices.

If you’re coming with others and making reservations separately, please give us your group’s name or point person in the Special Request box. We'll make seat you sit together!

FINDING US: We are across the street from the Outlet Collection (formerly known as The Super Mall) in Auburn, at 920 15th St SW. We are at the intersection you would turn into for the theatres. We have nearly 3000 sq ft of studio space and plenty of free parking. Look for our large illuminated Painting with a Twist Signs!

REFUND/CANCELLATION POLICY: We'd rather see you in the studio, but we know life happens! If you must cancel less than 24 hours notice, we are happy to provided a non-expiring gift certificate for you to use on your next visit. We cannot provide refunds or credits for No Shows.
